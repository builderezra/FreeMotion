Settings: Style, Shutter, Quality, Threshold, Softness. Featured in the effects carousel; works on video, images, shapes, text and groups; identical in export. Verified: symmetric 0→128→0 smear profile across a moving object's path, static content untouched, decaying echo trail, ~19ms/frame preview cost.
